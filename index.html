About us

Expect More Than Just a medicine

Welcome to Apotek SLS, where we are dedicated to providing top-tier pharmaceutical care to our community in Puri Surya Jaya, Jl. Athena Barat H3 No.9, Kabupaten Sidoarjo, Jawa Timur 61254. With almost a decade years of experience, our skilled team of pharmacists and healthcare professionals is committed to your well-being. We pride ourselves on our modern facility, equipped with cutting-edge technology, ensuring the accurate handling of your prescriptions and personalized medication consultations. Beyond healthcare, Apotek SLS actively engages in community health initiatives, striving to be your trusted partner in your health journey. Thank you for choosing Apotek SLS, where your health and community well-being are our utmost priorities.
